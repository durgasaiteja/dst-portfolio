How I Work

My Design Process

01

Discover & Define

I start by deeply understanding the problem space — stakeholder interviews, user research, competitive analysis, and data review. No assumptions, only evidence. This is how Ckids began — by sitting with parents and understanding their fears around screen time.

02

Ideate & Explore

Translating insights into concepts through rapid sketching, user flows, and information architecture. For Cognitivebotics, I ran co-creation sessions with therapists and caregivers to ensure the mental model matched the real world.

03

Design & Prototype

High-fidelity interfaces built with systems thinking — consistent components, accessible patterns, and micro-interactions that delight without distraction. ZipCart's full design system was built component-first before any screen was composed.

04

Test & Iterate

Usability testing with real users, synthesizing feedback, and refining. Great design is never finished — it evolves with the people who use it. Ckids went through 4 major UX iterations based on parent feedback before reaching its final form.
